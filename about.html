About Us

We are the oldest and the largest supporters club of Fc Goa- ESTD 2014. Having a presence all over Goa, we have been drumming up support for the Gaurs even before the first ball was kicked in the ISL. Having traveled the length & breadth of the country on #GaurTrips in the seasons gone by keeping the costs as low as possible making it possible for all to travel for Away matches, apart part from the various LIVE SCREENINGS & EVENTS we have been organizing in Goa. Let's Start a Revolution & live our dream of having a Footballing nation.
